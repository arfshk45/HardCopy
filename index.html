How HardCopy works..

Harcopy is a Lending and Borrowing platform, where you can onboard to lend and borrow books. As a Lender you can lend books, which are lying in your shelf form many days. Borrower can borrow books for least expenses instead of purchasing a new book for one time reading. You can lend and borrow books on weekly basis from our catalogue menu.

If you are a lender..

You will be sharing the books pictures with us and provide a rating from 1-10 (Book renting price will be decided based on version of book and price available in online and offline). Book will be collected and delivered according to the no.of days of ordered by the other customer, and amount you will calculated and decided according to the prices defined. Book pricing and Rating will be decide on the day of onboarding with Hardcopy and provide your books in our catalogue.

Minimal charges will be collected from the final amount according to T&C.
Rating 9-10: Newly purchased book
Rating 7-8: Should look like a new book with minimum folds
Rating5-6: Medium sized folds, marking with pen and pencils.
Ratiang below 5: Not suggested to rent.
Your book will be returned to you in the same condition while it was taking from you, If any damages or tears happen Harcopy is responsible to provide the book in same condition.

Your books safety is our main priority.
